Meet the Seven Sisters

The Seven Sisters, also known as the seven northeastern states of India, are a beautiful and culturally diverse part of the country. Each state, including Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura, offers a unique blend of traditions, languages, and customs that make the region vibrant and distinctive. Often referred to as India's hidden gems, these states are blessed with lush greenery, rolling hills, cascading waterfalls, and a rich biodiversity that draws nature lovers from around the world. The people of the Seven Sisters are known for their warm hospitality, strong cultural identity, and deep-rooted connection to their indigenous heritage. Despite being geographically isolated from the rest of India for many years, the northeastern states have preserved their cultural diversity while adapting to modern changes. Their festivals, music, dance, and art reflect a harmonious coexistence of traditional values and contemporary influences, making the region an ideal destination for those seeking both adventure and serenity. However, not a huge number of people are well aware of the cultures, virtues and heritage of these beautiful states.

ABOUT HORIZON SEVEN

Horizon Seven is a digital platform dedicated to showcasing the rich heritage, diverse culture, and breathtaking beauty of North East India, home to the Seven Sisters: Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura. Our mission is to bring the stories, traditions, and vibrant lives of these states into the spotlight, offering a deeper connection to the land, its people, and its history. We believe in preserving and promoting the cultural wealth of North East India by creating a bridge between these magnificent states and the rest of the world, helping people explore the unexplored and appreciate the timeless beauty of this region. Horizon Seven showcases a variety of aspects, including traditional art and craft, festivals and celebrations, cuisine, languages and literature, natural wonders, and the historical significance of the region. From ancient craft techniques and grand festivals to the linguistic diversity and stunning landscapes, we aim to provide comprehensive and authentic content. Our team collaborates with local historians, artisans, and cultural experts to ensure that each story is meaningful and true to the region. At Horizon Seven, we offer engaging content that informs and inspires, whether you're a traveler, historian, or someone looking to learn. Our goal is not only to celebrate the vibrant past of the Seven Sisters but also to embrace their evolving present, as these regions move forward while maintaining their unique identity. Join us on this incredible journey and discover the unparalleled heritage of North East India.
